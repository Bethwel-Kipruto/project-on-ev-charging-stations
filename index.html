OUR CHARGING STATIONS


Welcome to ALL THINGS EV, your go-to source for electric vehicle (EV) charging stations in Kenya. We are committed to building a robust network of charging stations to support the growing
adoption of EVs in the country. Our team of experts is dedicated to delivering exceptional customer service and ensuring that your EV charging experience is seamless and hassle-free. Join us in our mission to drive Kenya towards a cleaner and greener future.
